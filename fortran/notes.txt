Some notes about the fortran stuff:

Variables:
    - F_proj : forces on the rod projrcted on it's long axis
    - F_pRX : x component of longitudinal forces on rod
    - F_pRX : y component of longitudinal forces on Rod
    - velC_scf : velocity of particle along long axis of rod
    - velRod_scf : velocity of rod along it's long axis


Commands etc.:
    - .ge. : greater than or equal to
